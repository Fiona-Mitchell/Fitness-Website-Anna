Hi, I'm Anna!




I’m an exercise lover and plant-based foodie who’s passionate about promoting a more balanced, kind and inclusive approach to fitness and wellbeing.

We all have different backgrounds, lifestyles, ambitions and priorities. There is no 'one size fits all' approach when it comes to our wellbeing, and because of this I tailor everything around you, your individual lifestyle and goals.

Personally, I love the way exercise makes me feel and the mindset it puts me in. I do the things I enjoy, whether that’s dancing to my favourite tunes in my bedroom (or taking over the whole house…), weight training at the gym, going for a run in the sunshine, yin yoga or doing an online class and sweating it out alongside other people. There are so many ways to enjoy movement and I’m here to guide you to find what kind of movement sparks joy in your life right now. I hope that each session will leave you feeling empowered, strong (in whichever way the word strong means to you), accomplished and looking forward to the next!

Veganism and Fitness


The question which I get asked the most, especially within the fitness community is “where do you get your protein?”. I was also concerned about this when I first changed my diet, but little did I know that plants have so much protein too! Beans, rice, quinoa, oats, tofu, broccoli, nuts, seeds & soya products just to name a few! In fact, everything has a certain amount of protein in it.

Now that I train more regularly, I want to fuel my body appropriately and this is 100% possible on a plant based diet! I have honestly never felt better or had more energy. So I’d love to share with you what I’ve learnt over the years and help you achieve your fitness goals on a balanced, nourishing, delicious and abundant plant based lifestyle.
